FAQ

Is it safe for daily use?

Yes. The formula is 100% natural and designed for nightly use during a trip or sleep reset (up to a 6-day course).

Will I feel groggy in the morning?

No. GABA works with your body's own calming system instead of sedating it, so you wake up clear-headed.

Can I bring it on a flight?

Yes. No liquids, no restrictions — a patch is cabin-safe and TSA-friendly. Apply before takeoff.

Where do I stick it?

Clean, dry skin on the inner wrist, shoulder, or upper back. Rotate spots between uses.

How is this different from sleeping pills?

Patches avoid the stomach entirely, release slowly over 8 hours, and use natural actives — not sedatives.

Is it safe for sensitive skin?

The adhesive is latex-free and paraben-free. Patch-test on the inner forearm if you have very sensitive skin.
